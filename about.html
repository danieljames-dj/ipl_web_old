IEEE Programming League 2016

The IEEE PL is a team competition. Current rules stipulate that each team consist of three students. Participants must be undergraduate university students from India. The participants must attend all the tutorial webinars of ‘Programming in Ruby/Python’ and ‘Algorithm solving’ hosted by the organising team.

Mission: To create a platform for Indian student members of IEEE to provide training to excel in programming competitions like IEEE Xtreme, ACM ICPC etc.


History

IEEE Programming League, IEEE PL, traces its roots to the nationwide programming contest held in January 2015 hosted by Federal Institute of Science And Technology, FISAT in association with IEEE Computer Society – India Council. The program was held in three tiers – Computer Science Quiz, Decoding and Coding. The event saw participation of more than 400 teams (over 1200 participants) from universities across the country including prestigious Indian Institute of Technology’s (IIT’s).
